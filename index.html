Introduction

Clicker Heroes is an idle game made by Playsaurus. The game was originally released in 2014 for browsers, in 2015 for mobile devices, and 2017 for console.

The basic premise of the game revolves around killing monsters for gold, which can then be used to purchase heroes to attack monsters, killing them faster and earning more gold. Clicker Heroes is one of the most popular and successful idle games, with over 6 million players downloading the game on Steam, each player averaging 124 hours played on the game. The success of Clicker Heroes has inspired many other developers to produce similar games. Clicker Heroes itself was one of the many games that came out partially because of the success of the first hugely popular incremental game, Cookie Clicker, by Orteil.
