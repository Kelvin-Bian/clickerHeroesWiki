Skills

Introduction

Skills are unlocked through levelling up your heroes, and can be useful tools that will help progression through the game. There are currently a total of 9 skills that can be activated on a set cooldown after unlock. Upon activation, your heroes or clicks will increase in power for a set duration depending on the skill description.
