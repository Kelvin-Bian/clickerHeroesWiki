Heroes

Introduction

One of the core mechanics of Clicker Heroes is the heroes. Heroes are your primary source of damage, killing the monsters automatically (with the exception of the first hero, Cid, who only increases your click damage). Heroes can be recruited and leveled up with gold. Currently, there are 54 heroes in the game.

Heroes can be leveled up, increasing their total DPS by the base DPS of the hero. In addition to increasing their damage, leveling a hero also unlocks certain upgrades for the hero once they reach certain levels. Each time you level a hero up, its next level costs 6.975% more than the previous level, leading to an exponential increase in the cost to level up. Every hero gets a 4x increase in their damage every 25 levels from level 200 onwards, and a 10x increase in their damage every 1000 levels until level 8000. Heroes can be gilded, which will give them a 50% increase in damage (stackable additively). A random hero becomes gilded after beating 10 levels of monsters after level 100. Gilds can be transferred by either removing a gild off a hero and randomly assigning it to another hero (costs 2 Hero Souls), or choosing a hero to take a gild from another, randomly selected hero (costs 80 Hero Souls).
