Monsters

Introduction

An important feature of Clicker Heroes is the ability to kill enemy monsters through clicking or damage from your heroes. Upon killing a monster, it will drop gold, which is a currency that can be used to level up heroes or skills. Monsters belong to zones or numbered areas that increase in number as you progress through the game. To increase the level of your zone, it is necessary to defeat more challenging monsters. A higher zone level corresponds to more HP and gold dropped from its monsters. There are many different types of monsters throughout the game. Each has a unique name, sprite, and zone number.
